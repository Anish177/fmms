About Us

Our project has successfully developed a real-time system for monitoring fish behavior and environmental parameters using a Raspberry Pi, camera, and temperature and pH sensor. Through rigorous testing and validation, our system accurately measures water temperature and pH levels, while also detecting and classifying fish movements in real-time. With a user-friendly interface for viewing data and analysis, our system has diverse applications in research and commercial aquaculture. By harnessing AI object detection, our project showcases the potential of technology in revolutionizing fish behavior monitoring. Our innovative technologies contribute to the advancement of aquaculture research and education, promoting sustainability and efficiency in the industry.
